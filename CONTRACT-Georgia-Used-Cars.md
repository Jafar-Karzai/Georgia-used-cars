## 1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

The Developer agrees to design, develop, and deploy a comprehensive digital transformation platform for Georgia Used Cars (the "Platform"), as detailed in the project proposal dated November 9th, 2025.

### 1.1 Project Scope

The Platform includes the following core components:

- **Vehicle Inventory Management System** with 17-stage status tracking
- **Financial Management System** with UAE VAT compliance and expense tracking
- **Customer Relationship Management** with multi-channel inquiry management
- **Team Management & Access Control** with role-based permissions
- **Business Intelligence & Reporting** with real-time analytics
- **Professional Public Website** with vehicle showcase and WhatsApp integration
- **Admin Panel** with comprehensive management capabilities

Complete specifications are detailed in the attached Project Proposal document.
